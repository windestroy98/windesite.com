How to Turn Scratch Games to EXE Using TurboWarp

This article demonstrates how to convert Scratch games into executable files (EXE) using TurboWarp, allowing you to share and play your projects outside of the Scratch environment.

  1. Go to the TurboWarp editor by visiting https://turbowarp.org/editor.
  2. Upload your Scratch project from your device.
  3. Click on the "Packpage Project" button.
  4. Select the desired settings, such as framerate, icons, cloud variables, etc.
  5. Choose "Environment" as "Zip (ideal for websites)".
  6. Click the "Packpage" button.
  7. Wait for the download to complete.
  8. Once downloaded, Click the ZIP file.
  9. You now have a ZIP file, the EXE is inside, dont extract it
    10. Done, you now have an exe scratch game!
